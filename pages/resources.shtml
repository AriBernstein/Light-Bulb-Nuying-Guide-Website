Critique:

I had a lot of fun with this project. It was my first opportunity during my Web and Mobile 1 class to really incorporate imagery and graphic design into my code. I feel that this is the strongest aspect of the site. Otherwise, I enjoy the quirky animations, such as the header light bulb, which changes color on hover, as well as the parallax image on the home page.

I feel that the navigation can use improvement. It feels rather clunky and really misses potential. I am proud of it nonetheless as it was difficult to implement.
